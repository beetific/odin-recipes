Gigi Hadid Pasta

Description

Searching for the recipe for the Gigi Hadid recipe that's gone viral on TikTok? Look no further! Gigi's recipe is a twist on a traditional pasta with vodka sauce - but without the vodka and with a lot more red pepper flakes!

This recipe is delicious, filling, and easy to make - once your ingredients are prepped, everything should come together in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (of your choosing!)
  • Tomato paste
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Onions (chopped)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Minced garlic
  • Oregano
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
